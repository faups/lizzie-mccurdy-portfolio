Digital Media Production

From my time participating as a performer and technical producer in high school theater, to my internship at Cannes Film Festival and attending industry events at Sundance Film Festival, I found my passion. I thrive in the place where creativity, technology and business collide. As I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Digital Media Production, I aimed to transform that passion into performance. I have experience overseeing all areas of video production through a variety of projects, utilizing skills including but not limited to: administering budgets, maintaining calendars, talent selection, writing, directing, videography and editing. I am eager to continue exploring this industry through professional experience.
